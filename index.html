Origin

The history of coffee dates back to the 15th century, and possibly earlier with a number of reports and legends surrounding its first use. The earliest credible sources of coffee mentioned as a beverage comes from Sufi monasteries in Yemen in the 15th century, though there’s reason to believe non-Muslim Arabians had been making wine with the coffee cherries for over 200 years already.
